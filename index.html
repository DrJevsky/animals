About the Simulation

This ecosystem simulation features 5 different animal species with unique behaviors:

  • Rabbits - Fast breeding herbivores that feed on vegetation
  • Deer - Larger herbivores with better vision and slower reproduction
  • Foxes - Small predators that hunt rabbits
  • Wolves - Pack predators that hunt deer
  • Bears - Apex predators that can hunt any animal

Animals have health, age, hunger levels, and unique traits (speed, size, vision) that are inherited by offspring with some variation. Males and females must meet to reproduce. Watch as the ecosystem balances itself through predator-prey relationships!
